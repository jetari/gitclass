Melinda Gates

Empowering Women

Bill and Melinda started the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation in 2000, which includes a global Gender Equality strategy. The primary goal is to remove the barriers facing women and girls to ensure they have an equal chance to thrive and lead healthy, productive lives.
Melinda Gates started Pivotal Ventures in 2015 as an investment and incubation company working to drive social progress for women and families in the United States.
On October 5, 2019, Gates announced in an article she wrote for Time Magazine that her investment company Pivotal Ventures will donate $1 billion to gender equality
Melinda launched the website Evoke in March, 2019 as place to highlight innovative ideas and creative solutions from people who are committed to improving the world. The launched during Women's History Month, which was no coincidence according to Gates.

"I want to see more women in the position to make decisions, control resources, and shape policies and perspectives. I believe that women’s potential is worth investing in—and the people and organizations working to improve women’s lives are, too."

—Melinda Gates, Time Magazine
